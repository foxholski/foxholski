2/6/2003 - A couple of things... [Posted by Tom]
Howdy folks... a few of you have been complaining about the lack of updates to the site... well this is because if you are checking the site on the OLD hosting, it does not get updated anymore... as we have moved to our new hosting.  So if you've bookmarked the site before it was on the new hosting (the old hosting is Marks Tesco space), make sure you bookmark the new site (you can do that now by hitting Ctrl+D).

Secondly theres a group of people in Norwich that are planning on setting up some rehearsal studios - fantastic!  But, they need your help!  They're trying to get a grant from the council to help them out, but to get it they need letters and emails and anything like that from bands saying that there aren't enough rehearsal facilities in norwich (which there aren't of course).  So if you wanna help out, email Finn at fetcha@hotmail.com saying how we NEED more rehearsal facilities in Norwich.  Talk about this in the forums here.

Who is this imposter on Bargain Hunt?  You know this country is going down hill when you can't see David D. on Bargain Hunt.  Argh!
